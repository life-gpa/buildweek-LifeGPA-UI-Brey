Features of LifeGPA

Habit Tracker

Our Habit Tracker is the main-purpose of LifeGPA. We like to keep it as simple as possible for you to keep track of what you're accomplishing each and every day!

Custom Habits

You can certainly choose to follow the habits we have made for you. We have also made it possible for you to make you own habits, to customize the app to you!

Percentage Calculator

The LifeGPA app does the work for you! We will keep track of how many days you perform each habit, and give you the average for that habit. Habits are tracked in 30, 60, and 90 day periods.

Your Life GPA Score

LifeGPA will take the averages from all of your habits, average them together, and give you your GPA! One simple grade on how awesome you are at life!
