Welcome to LifeGPA

Are you dominating life the way you could be?
If you could give your life a grade, what would it be?
Well let's find out!

Statistically, it takes 66 consecutive days to develop a new habit. LifeGPA is your answer to making the next 66 days much easier!

How you do anything is how you do everything! Big success is made up of a lot of smaller steps. We will help you conquer those smaller steps to achieve the bigger end-goal.

LifeGPA takes all of your life's most important tasks, and summarizes them into one simple grade. We differ from all of the other 'tracking-apps' in one way. We give you one overall grade, rather than measuring progress in multile areas. The grading scale works just like your high school GPA. A 4.0 means you are crushing your tasks and goals. A 1.0 means you need to step it up! You can track any task or habit you want inside of LifeGPA! Professional goals, personal development, fitness, and relationships are a great place to start grading yourself! Do you have something specific you want to track? No problem! You can enter any task you'd like to grade yourself on and let LifeGPA do the rest!
