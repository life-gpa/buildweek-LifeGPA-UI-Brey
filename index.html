How It Works

What LifeGPA Does For You

LifeGPA takes all of your life's most important tasks, and summarizes them into one simple grade. We differ from all of the other 'tracking-apps' in one way. We give you one overall grade, rather than measuring progress in multile areas. The grading scale works just like your high school GPA. A 4.0 means you are crushing your tasks and goals. A 1.0 means you need to step it up! You can track any task or habit you want inside of LifeGPA! Professional goals, personal development, fitness, and relationships are a great place to start grading yourself! Do you have something specific you want to track? No problem! You can enter any task you'd like to grade yourself on and let LifeGPA do the rest!

How LifeGPA Works

In order to use LifeGPA, all you need to do is enter your information, and then enter the tasks you want to track. Simply enter each task you perform each day before you go to bed. LifeGPA will keep track of how many times you perform each task over a 30, 60, and 90 day period. It will average each habit, and then average all of your habits together for you life's GPA!

Features of LifeGPA

Habit Tracker

Our Habit Tracker is the main-purpose of LifeGPA. We like to keep it as simple as possible for you to keep track of what you're accomplishing each and every day!

Custom Habits

You can certainly choose to follow the habits we have made for you. We have also made it possible for you to make you own habits, to customize the app to you!

Percentage Calculator

The LifeGPA app does the work for you! We will keep track of how many days you perform each habit, and give you the average for that habit. Habits are tracked in 30, 60, and 90 day periods.

Your Life GPA Score

LifeGPA will take the averages from all of your habits, average them together, and give you your GPA! One simple grade on how awesome you are at life!
